### FAQ

**Q: What are the benefits of using alignment sticks during practice?**

Alignment sticks are useful training aids that help golfers improve their stance, alignment, and swing path. They can be placed on the ground to ensure proper body alignment to the target or used to visualize swing paths. This tool is ideal for players looking to develop consistency in their setup and overall accuracy.

**Q: How do winter golf balls differ from standard golf balls?**

Winter golf balls, often available in yellow for visibility in snowy conditions, are designed to perform better in colder temperatures. They typically have a softer cover, which helps maintain feel and performance on the greens even when temperatures drop, making them a suitable choice for winter play.

**Q: What advantages do 3-piece golf balls offer?**

3-piece golf balls consist of a core, an inner layer, and a cover, providing enhanced performance characteristics. The multi-layer construction allows for better control and spin on approach shots, while still offering distance off the tee. This makes them a preferred option for players seeking a balance between distance and feel.
