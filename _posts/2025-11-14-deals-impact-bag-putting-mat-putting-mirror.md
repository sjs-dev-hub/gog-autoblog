### FAQ

**Q: What is the primary benefit of using an impact bag for golfers?**

An impact bag helps golfers practice their swing mechanics by providing instant feedback on their impact position, allowing them to improve their contact with the ball and enhance their overall swing consistency.

**Q: How do putting mats compare to putting mirrors for practice?**

Putting mats simulate the experience of putting on a green and allow for distance and alignment practice, while putting mirrors assist in developing proper alignment and stroke path by providing visual feedback on the setup and stroke mechanics.

**Q: What should I consider when choosing between a high MOI driver and a game improvement driver?**

When selecting between a high MOI driver and a game improvement driver, consider your skill level and swing speed. High MOI drivers are designed to minimize twisting on off-center hits, making them suitable for golfers seeking more stability, while game improvement drivers often feature enhanced forgiveness and larger sweet spots, ideal for those looking to elevate their performance.
