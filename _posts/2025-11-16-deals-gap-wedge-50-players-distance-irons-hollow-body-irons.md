### FAQ

**Q: What is the main purpose of a gap wedge, and how does it compare to other wedges?**

A gap wedge, typically lofted between 50 and 54 degrees, is designed to fill the distance gap between a pitching wedge and a sand wedge. It offers a versatile option for approach shots and short game situations, providing increased accuracy and control around the greens compared to higher-lofted wedges.

**Q: How do players distance irons differ from traditional irons?**

Players distance irons are engineered to offer a balance of distance and accuracy, featuring a larger sweet spot and often incorporating game-improvement technology. Unlike traditional irons, they provide added forgiveness on off-center hits, making them suitable for golfers seeking both distance and playability without sacrificing feel.

**Q: What benefits does a golf launch monitor provide for improving performance?**

A golf launch monitor tracks various metrics such as ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and carry distance. This data allows golfers to analyze their swing mechanics and make informed adjustments to their technique, helping to enhance overall performance, club selection, and shot consistency on the course.
