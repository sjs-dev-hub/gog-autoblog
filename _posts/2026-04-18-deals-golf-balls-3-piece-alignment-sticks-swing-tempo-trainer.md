### FAQ

**Q: What are the benefits of using a 3-piece golf ball?**

A 3-piece golf ball typically offers a soft feel, enhanced spin control, and improved distance. The construction usually features a core for distance, a mantle layer for spin, and a cover for durability and feel, making it suitable for players looking for a balanced performance.

**Q: How can alignment sticks improve my golf game?**

Alignment sticks help golfers ensure proper aim and body alignment during practice. They can be used for setting up shots, refining swing paths, and improving overall consistency in stance and alignment, which are crucial for accurate ball striking.

**Q: What is the purpose of a swing tempo trainer?**

A swing tempo trainer aids golfers in developing a consistent swing rhythm and timing. By promoting the correct tempo, these trainers can help improve swing mechanics, resulting in better ball striking and overall performance on the course.
