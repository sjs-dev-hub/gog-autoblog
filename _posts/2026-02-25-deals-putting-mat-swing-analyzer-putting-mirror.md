### FAQ

**Q: What is the primary purpose of a putting mat in golf training?**

A putting mat is designed to simulate the feel of a real green, allowing golfers to practice their putting stroke indoors or in limited spaces. It helps improve accuracy and consistency in putting by providing a controlled surface for regular practice.

**Q: How does a swing analyzer enhance a golfer's performance?**

A swing analyzer uses sensors and technology to track and analyze various aspects of a golfer's swing, such as speed, angle, and path. This data helps golfers identify areas for improvement, enabling them to make necessary adjustments to enhance their performance on the course.

**Q: What are the benefits of using a putting mirror during practice?**

A putting mirror provides visual feedback on a golfer's alignment and stroke mechanics. By allowing golfers to see their stance, eye position, and stroke path in real-time, it aids in developing a more consistent putting technique and promotes better overall performance on the green.
