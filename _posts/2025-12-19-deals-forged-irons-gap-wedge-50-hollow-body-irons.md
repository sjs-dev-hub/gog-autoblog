### FAQ

**Q: What are the advantages of using forged irons in my golf game?**

Forged irons are known for their superior feel and control, offering a more refined feedback on strikes. They are often favored by skilled players who prioritize precision and shot shaping, as these irons can provide more consistent performance around the green.

**Q: How does a gap wedge (50 degrees) fit into my golf club set?**

A gap wedge, typically around 50 degrees of loft, serves as a bridge between your pitching wedge and sand wedge. It helps golfers manage shorter approach shots and delicate chips, providing versatility for various lie conditions and distances.

**Q: What benefits do hollow body irons offer compared to traditional cavity-backed irons?**

Hollow body irons feature a hollow construction that enhances forgiveness and increases distance. This design allows for a lower center of gravity, which can help golfers achieve a higher launch angle and increased ball speed, making them suitable for players looking for added distance without sacrificing control.
