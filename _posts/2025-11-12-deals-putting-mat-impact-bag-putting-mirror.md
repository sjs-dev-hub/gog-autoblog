### FAQ

**Q: What is the primary purpose of using a putting mat in golf practice?**

A putting mat is designed to simulate real putting greens, allowing golfers to practice their stroke and distance control at home or indoors. It provides a consistent surface for honing putting skills and can help improve accuracy.

**Q: How does an impact bag benefit my golf swing?**

An impact bag is used to enhance the golfer's understanding of the correct impact position in their swing. It provides instant feedback on swing mechanics and helps develop muscle memory for a solid strike, ultimately improving ball contact and consistency.

**Q: Why would I consider using a putting mirror during my practice sessions?**

A putting mirror is a valuable training aid that helps golfers visualize their alignment and stroke mechanics. By allowing players to see their eye position and body alignment relative to the ball and target line, it aids in developing a more consistent putting stroke.
